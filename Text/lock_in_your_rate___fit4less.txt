Home Gym Memberships Lock in Your Rate Don't Wait, Lock in Your Rate! Lock in Your Rate and your membership fee will never increase. That means for as long as you’re a member, you’ll always pay the same low price for your membership. How do I Lock in My Rate? All you have to do is opt-in! By locking in your rate, you’re ensuring that your original membership rate never increases for as long as you maintain your membership. A fee of $9.95+tax is charged on January 1st, April 1st, July 1st and October 1st. There is no better time than right now to make sure your rates won't change—it's like a security blanket for your gym membership! Ready to Lock in Your Rate? Simply fill out the form on our Membership Changes page and select the Lock In My Membership Rate option in the Action Required dropdown. LOCK IN MY RATE NOW! *By filling out the Membership Changes form and selecting Lock in My Membership Rate, you are agreeing to payments of $9.95+tax every three months. These payments are non-refundable (including during the 30-day cancellation period). You can opt-out at any time by giving notice in-club with at least 12 business days’ notice before the next payment would be taken. These payments will automatically stop upon cancellation of the membership, when notice of cancellation is received 30 business days prior to the withdrawal.
