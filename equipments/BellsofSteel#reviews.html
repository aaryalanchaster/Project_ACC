Why Buy From Us

Bells of Steel’s mission is to help you get stronger, healthier and more muscles for your hard-earned dollars. We do this by providing real value to YOU, with intelligently designed, best bang for your buck fitness equipment.

Our knowledgeable customer service team will help you build your dream gym in the comfort of your home via live chat, phone or email. And our weekly educational videos on YouTube will help keep you on track to crush all of your fitness goals.

We ship your order out the same or the next day to anywhere in Canada from our 30,000 sq ft warehouse in Calgary or our 15,000 sq ft Toronto. We use top-of-the-line packaging to make sure your order arrives safe and sound, and you get a tracking number as soon as it ships.

If for whatever reason you don’t love your new equipment you can use our 30-day money-back guarantee to return your order, we’ll even pay the shipping back! And our industry-leading warranty will ensure you love your Bells of Steel equipment for decades to come.

With over 8,000 reviews and 24,000 Canadians served since 2010, we can’t wait to serve you and your fitness goals for years to come.

Now let the GAINZ begin!
