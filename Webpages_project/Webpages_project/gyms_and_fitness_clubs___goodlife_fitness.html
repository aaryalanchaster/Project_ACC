Featured Amenities

Youll find these featured amenities and so many others across our Clubs. Interested in a specific location? Find a Club above to see all of the amenities it has to offer.

GROUP FITNESS

Whether youre a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, weve got classes for you. Many of our locations feature Cycling and Yoga Studios, in addition to our regular Exercise Studio. Our Cardio, Strength, Mind & Body and Cycling Classes will get you fit and keep you motivated!

REGYMEN

DISRUPT. YOUR. ROUTINE. With REGYMEN, you dont simply get classesyou get results. Give us 60-minutes of your day and well give you the body youve always wanted. Our unique combination of HIIT and dynamic strength training techniques encourage the afterburn effect, so your body will continue to burn calories even after the workout is done. 

 

PERSONAL TRAINING

GoodLife certified Personal Trainers are here to help you achieve more than you ever thought possible! No matter what your needs or fitness goals are, we have a custom Personal Training package that's right for you.

HOT YOGA

Where mind, body and fitness merge. A series of yoga postures practiced in a warm and humid environment. The heat allows your muscles to stretch deeper and more effectively with less danger of injury, while detoxifying and cleansing your body through sweat.

TURF ZONE

Train like an athlete on the turf! The Turf Zone is a great area to try out strength and conditioning exercises that require more space. Plus, it gives you the opportunity to train on a surface similar to what youd compete on.

VIRTUAL FITNESS CLASSES

Virtual Fitness is now available across Canada. Classes are held in either the Group Fitness or Cycling Studios via a projector and screen during off-peak hours. Virtual Fitness is perfect for those days when you need a workout that fits your schedule!

Land Acknowledgement

GoodLife Fitness was founded on the traditional lands of the Anishinaabek (Ah-nish-in-a-bek), Haudenosaunee (Ho-den-no-show-nee), Lnaapewak (Len-ahpay- wuk) and Attawandaron (Add-a-won-da-run) Peoples, on lands connected with the London Township and Sombra Treaties of 1796 and the Dish with One Spoon Covenant Wampum. This land, and the land on which all GoodLife Fitness Clubs operate, continues to be home to diverse Indigenous Peoples (First Nations, Inuit and Mtis) whom we recognize as contemporary stewards of the land and vital contributors of our society.
